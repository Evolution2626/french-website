2013 Robot

  • 2013 Industrial Design Award

This time, the robot ahve to play Frisbee. For scoring points, the robots launch frisbee in the goals placed at each end of the field or in the goal at the top of the pyramid. The points are given this way : 3 points for the highest goal of the field, 2 points for the middle goal, 1 point for the lowest goal and 5 points for the goal on the top of the pyramid. Moreover, if the frisbees get in the goals during de autonomous period, the points will be doubled. The field is seperate in two and in each part, there is a pyramid with three level. Near the end of the match, at 30 seconds, a robot can climb the pyramid. The climbing points are given in fonction of the level the robot reached. 10 points are given for the first level, 20 points for the second and 30 points for the the highest and final level.
