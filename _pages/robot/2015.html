2015 Robot

  • 2015 Engineering Inspiration Award

The challenge of this year is to pick up and stack totes on scoring platforms, put pool noodles inside recycling containers, and put containers on top of scoring stacks of totes. There is also a coopetition aspect of the game where both alliances of teams can pool their totes and stack them on a step dividing the field to each gain 20 points.
